How to Play

• Get your team ready!
This is a 3 Player Game, make sure all of you play at the same time! Each of the players will have a separate login code provided by us and different puzzles to solve.

Once you choose a box, you will have to make payment and submit your email id. Upon payment confirmation, an email with the login codes for all 3 players will be sent to the submitted email in 5-6 hours time.

• Escape the Box, together!
Once you have your team members and team login code ready (via email), its time to get your Grey Matter working! Get in on a Video Call with your team members, via Zoom, MS Teams, G-Meet or Skype to discuss your game plan!
Important: Each player must use only their respective logins and must not share screen !
