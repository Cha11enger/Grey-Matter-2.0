How to Play

◆ Get you team ready to login at the same time!
◆ Login using your Team Name and the unique Player 3 login code provided via email. We recommend playing in a Laptop/PC.
◆ Get into a Video call with your team to decide your Game Plan!
◆ DO NOT share your screen with your teammates, you can describe it to them though!
◆ The Hints button is available in the bottom left corner of your screen. Use only if absolutely necessary.
◆ If you are stuck at any point, drop us an email or text for hints/issues.
◆ Once any member of your team logs in, the clock starts ticking! Only the top teams will make it into out leader board.

Story Line

Three archaeologists discover a mysterious tomb during an excavation. As they explore the 5000 year old ruins, they find that it belong ot the evil tyrant 'Blood King' Groyder. While searching the ruins, they accidentally activate a trap and find themselves separated and locked in the three different chambers! Player 1: The Crimson Knight's Chamber Player 2: The Dark Mage's Chamber Player 3: The Emperor's Chamber
Can they work together to solve the puzzles with the clues being scattered across the chambers and escape the Pyramid? Or will they be cursed and trapped forever in the ruins?!
